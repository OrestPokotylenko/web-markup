Grote Markt

Would you like to see beautiful architecture?

First place you should visit in Haarlem is Grote Markt. It is a beautiful old part of the city that exists since 14th century. Grote Markt is full of old architecture. The most beautiful part of this place is an old St.Bavokerk church. At Grote Markt you can also find a lot of cafes where you can have a meal or just drink some coffee and admire the nice view.

How to get to Grote Markt?

Grote Markt is really easy to find. It is placed closed to central station in Haarlem. You can try to see the St.Bavokerk from there and just go to the church. You can also open google maps and walk for less than one kilometre to the Grote Markt.
